About Chosen Holistic Services

Who We Are

A Neighbour Who Cares

Chosen Holistic Services was born out of a simple belief: that everyone deserves access to products that support their health, and transportation that keeps them connected to the world around them.

Based in Saskatchewan, we set out to serve our surrounding community with something real — carefully chosen supplements, vitamins, and natural cleaning products that you can feel good about bringing into your home.

We also recognized that getting to the city isn't always easy. Whether it's a medical appointment in Prince Albert, a shopping trip, or plans in Saskatoon, our shuttle service was created to make that journey stress-free and affordable.

We are more than a business. We are part of this community, and everything we do is in service of the people in it.

Our Mission

"To support whole-person wellness and strengthen community connection through quality products and dependable services."

We believe wellness is holistic — it touches your body, your home, and your ability to participate in the community around you.
